How To use Snapfolio

1. Download the app - we are available on the App Store and Google Play.

Log in using Facebook

2. Upload an image. Make sure to use a clear image for better recognition

3.Tap inside a square to select object

4.Add object(s) from results to your Library.

5.Create your Library - You are all set!
